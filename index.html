Welcome to this glorious page

Hi there. My name is Josh Cough and I am a functional programmer who enjoys teaching programming, and who occassionally dabbles in musical theater on the side.

As far as programming goes, I'm very passionate about the Haskell programming language, and I'm currenly running Boston Haskell (which you can link to on the right). I'm probably best known for my work in Scala though, which I used to write about extensively on my blog. I've also given a number of talks at meetups and conferenes, and I'm in the process of linking to them on the right. Bear with me as that happens.

Besides Haskell and Scala, I write code in just about every language, but here are a few of my favorites and/or languages on my radar: Coq, Idris, Agda, Racket, Clojure, Ermine (which I helped develop), F#, and more.

I've had major roles in three musicals at the MIT Musical Theater Guild. Those shows are also linked to on the right.
